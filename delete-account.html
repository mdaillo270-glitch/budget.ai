Account & Data Deletion – Budget AI

This page explains how users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data for the Budget AI mobile application.

If you would like to permanently delete your Budget AI account and related data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

You may request deletion using one of the following methods:

  1. Inside the App (if available):
    Open Budget AI → Go to Settings → Select Delete Account → Confirm deletion.

  2. By Email Request:
    Send an email to: support@budgetai.app
    Subject line: Account Deletion Request
    Include your registered email address associated with your account.

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification of your request, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • User profile information (name, email, account details)
  • Budget records and expense entries
  • Uploaded files or attachments
  • App activity history
  • Authentication credentials

Data Retention & Exceptions

Certain information may be retained temporarily for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring
  • Legal compliance obligations
  • Financial or audit requirements (if applicable)

Any retained data will be securely stored and permanently deleted within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days after identity verification.

If you have any questions regarding account or data deletion, please contact us at emkeytechnologies@gmail.com.
