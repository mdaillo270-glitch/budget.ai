Privacy Policy

Gestion Budget & Dépenses - IA

Effective Date: February 14, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 14, 2026

Welcome to Gestion Budget & Dépenses - IA ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), the Google Play Developer Program Policies, and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in via Google or Apple.
  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, budgets, savings goals, and transaction details you enter into the App.
  • Company/Team Data: Company name, employee names, email addresses, and phone numbers if you use team features.
  • Chat Messages: Messages you send to the AI assistant within the App.
  • Profile Preferences: Language, currency, theme, and financial goals.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and app version.
  • Usage Data: Features used, screens visited, interaction timestamps, and crash reports.
  • Location Data: Approximate location for attendance/check-in features, only when you explicitly grant permission.
  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the App.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: Basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) from your authentication provider.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and services.
  • Process and display your financial data (expenses, budgets, missions, quotes).
  • Power AI features including the chat assistant, financial reports, and bill scanning.
  • Send push notifications for budget alerts, mission updates, and daily reports (with your consent).
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App (anonymized data only).
  • Comply with legal obligations.
AI Processing: When you use the AI chat or bill scanner, your messages and scanned content are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We do not use your data to train AI models. OpenAI processes data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: For optional features like push notifications, location access, and AI chat.
  • Contract Performance: To provide the services you signed up for.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve the App, prevent fraud, and ensure security.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Firebase / Google Cloud: For authentication, database storage, cloud functions, analytics, and push notifications.
  • OpenAI: AI chat messages and scanned bill content are processed by OpenAI's API.
  • Team Members: If you use team/company features, relevant financial data is shared with team members you authorize.
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Financial Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Chat History: AI chat messages are retained for up to 90 days for context, then automatically deleted.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.

6. Your Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

You have the right to:

  • Know: What personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights.

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: emkeyindustries@gmail.com

We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in.
  • Firestore Security Rules restricting data access to authorized users.
  • Secure storage for sensitive data on your device.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at emkeyindustries@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States (Google Cloud, OpenAI). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required by the GDPR.

11. Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies. Firebase Analytics may use device identifiers for anonymous usage tracking. You can opt out of analytics in your device settings.
